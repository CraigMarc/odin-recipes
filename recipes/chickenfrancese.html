Chicken Franchese

Description

Chicken Francese is an easy gourmet chicken recipe pan fried in an egg wash with a delicious white wine and butter lemon sauce.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Garlic Powder
  • Butter
  • White Wine
  • Chicken Broth
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Heat oil and prepare chicken
  2. Dredge the chicken and cook
  3. Make sauce
  4. Add chicken to sauce
